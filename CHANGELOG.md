# Changelog

All notable changes to the AI Governance Executive Guide Series will be documented in this file.

The format is based on [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.1.0/).

## [1.0.0] - 2025-12-21

### Added

#### Volume 1: Financial Services
- Complete AI governance framework aligned with SR 11-7
- Four-tier risk classification (Critical, Elevated, Standard, Minimal)
- Two governance tracks: Decisioning AI and Communications AI
- Third-party AI risk management guidance
- GenAI-specific controls for financial institutions
- 90-day implementation roadmap
- IG/examiner audit preparation checklists

#### Volume 2: Healthcare
- Clinical AI governance framework
- FDA SaMD regulatory alignment
- Patient safety and clinical workflow integration
- Two governance tracks: Clinical Decisioning AI and Clinical Communications AI
- EHR integration considerations
- Quality and Safety Committee structures
- Clinical AI incident response procedures

#### Volume 3: Federal Programs
- AI governance framework for federal program offices
- OMB M-24-10 implementation guidance
- PWS/SOW clause templates for AI requirements
- Contractor oversight and verification procedures
- "Governed Unit" definition for contract clarity
- AI System Suspension Authority clause
- IG audit readiness documentation
- NARA records schedule alignment for AI-generated content

#### Volume 4: Government Contractors
- Competitive positioning through governance readiness
- Contract clause response guide
- Evidence package structure and table of contents
- Day 1 / Day 30 / Day 90 delivery timeline
- Subcontractor AI governance management
- Proposal Pack Insert (copy/paste ready)
- "What NOT to Claim" credibility protection guidance
- Proposal differentiator language templates

### Technical Foundation
- All guides built on Four Tests Standard (4TS) principles
- Consistent tiering methodology across industries
- Evidence-based governance approach throughout
